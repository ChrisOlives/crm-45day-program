## DAY 20

### Objective
Build a HubSpot lead scoring model for SaaS (combining behavioral + demographic signals) and simulate a complete inbound funnel.

### Real-World Context
SaaS lead scoring is more sophisticated than real estate scoring because behavioral data is richer — product usage, page views, email clicks, feature requests. A RevOps specialist at a SaaS company who can build a multi-dimensional scoring model and connect it to sales routing is extremely valuable. This exact skill is listed in 80% of RevOps Manager job descriptions.

### Mini Theory (10 min)
Read: Fit score vs. engagement score (two-dimensional lead scoring). Fit = how well they match your ICP (Ideal Customer Profile) — company size, industry, role. Engagement = what they've done — opened emails, visited pricing page, used product. Why combining both gives you better SQL prediction than using either alone.

### Main Project Task
**Build the Stackr Two-Dimensional Lead Scoring System**

**Fit Score Attributes (using HubSpot Score OR manual property):**
(Create a second HubSpot Score clone using a calculated property — or use HubSpot's native score and document the logic)

Positive Fit Signals (+points):
- Industry = Technology or Marketing or Consulting: +20
- Number of Employees = 10–50: +15
- Number of Employees = 51–200: +10
- Job Title contains "Manager" OR "Director" OR "Head of": +15
- Job Title contains "CEO" OR "Founder" OR "COO": +20
